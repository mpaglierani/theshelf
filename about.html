About

The Shelf is a hub of comprehensive media reviews and captivating insights created by an English major with a passion for creative writing. With a deep appreciation for the written word and a love for exploring various forms of art, Marguerite provides thoughtful and engaging analysis of TV shows, movies, and books.

As an avid reader, writer, and music enthusiast, Marguerite brings a well-rounded perspective to each review, offering nuanced critiques and highlighting the artistic merits of different forms of media. Whether you're seeking recommendations for your next binge-worthy TV series, thought-provoking films, or literary gems, she's has you covered.

Drawing on personal experiences and a cherished connection to Friday and Saturday movie nights with parents, Marguerite understands the excitement of discovering new releases or rediscovering beloved classics. This nostalgic perspective adds a warm and relatable touch to the reviews, creating an inviting atmosphere for readers to reminisce and explore fresh entertainment options.
