Must-See Movies Coming Out in October (2023)

As the leaves change and the weather cools down, there's no better way to embrace the autumn season than with a trip to the movie theater. October is jam-packed with a diverse array of film releases, promising something for every cinephile. From spine-tingling horrors to heartwarming dramas and everything in between, here's a sneak peek at some of the must-see movies hitting the big screen this October.

Here's a rundown of the top 10 new releases you won't want to miss:

1. "Five Nights at Freddy's": Based on the video game franchise, a troubled security guard finds that his job won't be as easy as it seems.

2. "The Killer": Directed by David Fincher, and based on French graphic novel, a cold blooded killer begins to wonder if he is losing his mind as he lurks in the shadows.

3. "Priscilla": A look on Priscilla Beaulieu's relationship with Elvis Presley and what supposedly happened behind closed doors.

4. "Killers of the Flower Moon": A member of the Osage Nation tries to save her community from a killing spree fueled by oil and greed.

5. "Foe": An unvitied stranger shows up to Hen and Junior's door with a proposal that will throw their quiet life upside down.

6. "The Exorcist: Believer": A continuation of the beloved classic, Victor Fielding seeks out Chris MacNeil when his daughter and her friend show signs of demonic possession.

7. "Totally Killer": An infamous killer returns on Halloween night to claim a fourth victim 35 years later.

8. "Taylor Swift: The Era's Tour": A concert film following Pop sensation Taylor Swift on her groundbreaking Era's Tour across the United States.

9. "Dumb Money": Based on the true events, every day people flip the script on Wall Street by raising the price of GameStop stock.

10. "Pain Hustlers": After losing her job, a single mother takes a chance on a pharmecutical start up, but quickly discovers what is hiding underneath.
